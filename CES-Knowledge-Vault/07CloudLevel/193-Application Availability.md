## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

Application Availability is a quality attribute measuring the system's capacity to be operational and respond correctly within agreed thresholds. It combines uptime measurement (time-based) with request success rate (event-based), governed by SLOs/SLIs, and is enabled through architectural tactics that maximize MTBF and minimize MTTR.
