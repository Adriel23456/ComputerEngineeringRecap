## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

Latency is a quality attribute quantifying the time between a client's request and receipt of a useful response. As a key driver of user experience and system behavior, it must be measured by percentiles (not averages), managed through decomposition of its components, and governed by explicit SLO targets tied to business requirements.
