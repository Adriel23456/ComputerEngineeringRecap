## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

Microservices Architecture is a style that structures an application as a collection of loosely coupled, independently deployable services, each owning its data and implementing a specific business capability. It enables organizational scaling, technology flexibility, and independent deployment, with trade-offs in distributed systems complexity.
