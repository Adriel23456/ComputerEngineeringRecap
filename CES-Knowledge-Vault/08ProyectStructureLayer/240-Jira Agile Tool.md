## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

Jira is Atlassian's industry-leading project management tool designed for Agile software development teams. It supports Scrum and Kanban methodologies, providing features for backlog management, sprint planning, issue tracking, and reporting. Jira integrates with the entire Atlassian ecosystem (Confluence, Bitbucket, Trello) and numerous third-party tools, making it one of the most widely adopted Agile project management platforms.
