## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

The Daily Scrum is a 15-minute time-boxed event for the Development Team to synchronize activities and create a plan for the next 24 hours. It is held at the same time and place each day to reduce complexity. The Daily Scrum optimizes team collaboration and performance by inspecting work since the last Daily Scrum and forecasting upcoming Sprint work. It is not a status meeting for managers but a planning meeting for the Development Team.

### Purpose and Benefits

Synchronization:
- Everyone knows what others are working on
- Identifies collaboration opportunities
- Reveals impediments early

Planning:
- Creates daily work plan
- Adjusts based on new information
- Maintains Sprint momentum

Transparency:
- Progress visible to all
- Problems surface quickly
- No surprises at Sprint end

### Best Practices

| Practice | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Stand up | Keeps meeting short |
| Face the board | Focus on work, not people |
| Same time/place | Builds routine |
| 15 min max | Respects time |
| Team-run | Builds ownership |
| Parking lot | Defer detailed discussions |
