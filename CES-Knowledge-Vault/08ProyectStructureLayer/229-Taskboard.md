## ğŸ“š Idea/Concept

A Taskboard (also known as Scrum Board or Kanban Board) is a visual tool for managing and tracking work during a Sprint. Typically organized in columns representing workflow states (To Do, In Progress, Testing, Done), it allows the team to visualize progress, identify bottlenecks, and coordinate daily work. Each task or User Story is represented as a card that moves through columns as it advances. The Taskboard promotes transparency, facilitates team self-organization, and is the focal point during Daily Scrums.

### Physical vs Digital Boards

| Aspect | Physical Board | Digital Board |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Visibility | High (on wall) | Requires screen |
| Tactile | Satisfying to move cards | Less engaging |
| Remote work | Not possible | Essential |
| History | Manual tracking | Automatic |
| Metrics | Manual calculation | Auto-generated |
| Customization | Unlimited | Tool-dependent |
| Cost | Cheap | Subscription |
| Examples | Wall + sticky notes | Jira, Azure DevOps, Trello |
